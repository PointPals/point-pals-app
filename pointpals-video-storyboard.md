# PointPals — Video Storyboard for Test Group (Veo 2–Safe)

> **Runtime:** 5 scenes × 10 seconds = 50 seconds
> **Format:** Google Vids / Veo 2 with overlay workflow
> **Vibe:** Warm, everyday NZ homes with animated sticker-style companion characters interacting in the scene.
>
> **How it works:** Shoot real footage of NZ families doing normal things. Then use Veo 2 (or an equivalent animation tool) to generate the companion characters as animated overlay elements, composited into the scene during editing. The prompts below are for Veo 2's text-to-video — they describe what each shot should look like, keeping descriptions general and avoiding any policy triggers around minors, bedrooms, or age specificity.

## Scene 1 — Kitchen, Morning (10s)

### What the final edit will show
A family kitchen in the morning. A parent pours cereal, another packs lunchboxes. A child runs through. On the bench sits a glass jar with colourful marbles. A small sage-green animated character with a gold star badge appears beside the jar and drops one shiny marble in. The parent glances at the jar and smiles.

### Veo 2 Prompt — Kitchen Scene

```
A bright warm family kitchen in the morning, shot from a static camera angle near the counter. Golden sunlight through a window. An adult in their 30s with dark hair tied back pours cereal into bowls at the counter. Another adult stands nearby packing lunch containers. A young child runs past the frame, laughing. Visible on the kitchen bench is a clear glass jar about 20 cm tall, half-filled with colourful glass marbles in pastel shades of yellow, blue, green and pink. A two-dimensional animated character appears beside the jar — soft rounded chunky body in sage green, with a small gold star emblem on its chest. The character lifts one shiny gold marble in its tiny hands and drops it into the jar. The marble hits others with a small motion. The adult at the counter glances over and smiles warmly. The scene feels warm and lived-in, with a New Zealand home aesthetic — wooden benchtop, tiled splashback, morning light.
```

> **Shoot this:** Real family kitchen, morning, cereal bowls and lunchboxes, jar in shot
> **Animate overlay:** Bramble character + marble drop

### Audio
Morning ambience — water running, cutlery sounds, quiet laughter. A clear bell-like chime when the marble lands.

A warm sand-coloured animated character with floppy ear-tufts and big dot eyes sits on the cushion beside them, leaning in to look at the book. > **Shoot this:** Child on living room couch reading a book, daytime

| Child in pyjamas on a bed | Child in everyday clothes on a living room couch |

| "glowing marble" / "magical sparkle" | "shiny marble" / "sparkles flicker" |
| "real mother" / "real father" / "real family" | "an adult" / "a parent" / "a family" |

| "slow motion" | Removed entirely |
| "kids cheering, jumping up and down" | "child claps happily" |

## Production Workflow

1. **Shoot the 4 real scenes** — kitchen, garden, living room reading, living room reward
2. **Generate companion animations** separately using Veo 2 prompts above (with companion PNGs attached as reference images)
3. **Composite** in your video editor — overlay the companion character footage onto the real scenes, positioned at the described locations
4. **Add audio** — sounds downloaded from ElevenLabs (marble chime, wah-wah, theme song)
5. **Add end card** and text overlays

This approach keeps the companion designs consistent and lets you position them precisely in each scene.
